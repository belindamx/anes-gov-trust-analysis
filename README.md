## Trust in Government by Party Affiliation (ANES 2024)
This analysis examines whether Democrats and Republicans differed in their trust in the U.S. federal government in 2024, using data from the American National Election Studies (ANES). Trust is an ordinal variable and compared across parties using a Wilcoxon rank-sum test (hypothesis of comparisons), along with a nonparametric effect size (rank-biserial correlation).

**Data source:** American National Election Studies (ANES), 2024 Time Series
